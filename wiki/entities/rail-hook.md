# Rail Hook（墙板轨道挂钩）

## Summary（摘要）

EN: A small J-shaped hanging hook in champagne-gold metal that clips tool-free onto a slatwall-style metal rail inset in wall paneling. It is the replication target of this project. The top "mounting claw" is the precision interface that engages the rail — its dimensions are annotated in the source photos and must be reproduced exactly. Per the user (2026-07-09), the overall height and the protruding J-hook are variable parts that may be adapted freely. See [hook-replication-requirements](../topics/hook-replication-requirements.md).
ZH: 一个香槟金色金属的小型 J 形挂钩，无需工具即可扣到嵌在墙板里的 slatwall（槽板）式金属挂条上。它是本项目的复刻对象。顶部的 mounting claw（安装爪）是与挂条配合的精密接口 —— 其尺寸在来源照片中有标注，必须精确复刻。用户已明确（2026-07-09）：总高与外伸的 J 钩是可变部分，可以自由调整。见 [hook-replication-requirements](../topics/hook-replication-requirements.md)。

## Construction（构造）

EN: The hook is almost certainly a slice cut from a long aluminum extrusion: the entire shape is a constant 2D profile swept along the hook's width, the front serration ridges run along that width axis, and the cut side faces show uniform machining marks. `hook-mount-dimensions.jpg` is literally the extrusion cross-section seen end-on. Consequence for replication: getting the 2D profile right (especially in the claw region) captures the entire fit; width is a separate single number.
ZH: 这个挂钩几乎可以肯定是从一根长 aluminum extrusion（铝挤出型材）上切下的一段：整个形状是一个恒定的 2D profile（二维截面）沿挂钩宽度方向扫掠而成，正面锯齿棱沿宽度方向延伸，切割侧面有均匀加工纹。`hook-mount-dimensions.jpg` 就是沿挤出轴方向看到的端面截面。对复刻的意义：把 2D 截面（尤其爪部区域）做准就抓住了全部配合关系；宽度只是另外一个独立数字。

EN: Descriptive coordinate convention for later reference (not a plan): the profile plane is the vertical plane perpendicular to the rail; +X points away from the wall (room side), +Y points up; the extrusion/width direction runs along the rail.
ZH: 为后续引用约定的描述性坐标（不是计划）：profile 平面为垂直于挂条的竖直平面；+X 指向远离墙面（房间一侧），+Y 向上；挤出/宽度方向沿挂条延伸。

## Profile, top to bottom（截面自上而下）

- EN: **Top cap** — a 0.5 mm tall cap at the plate top, thickened to 3.0 mm total by protruding 0.8 mm toward the wall (D15). It bears on the wall panel above the rail and stabilizes the hook against over-rotation. (The earlier "slanted top edge" reading is superseded — see below.)
  ZH: **顶部小帽** —— 主板顶部高 0.5 mm 的小帽，向墙面凸出 0.8 mm、总厚 3.0 mm（D15）。抵在挂条上方的墙板面上，防止挂钩过度转动、辅助受力。（早先“顶边斜切”的解读已废弃 —— 见下文。）
- EN: **Front face, serrated patch** — fine horizontal ridges (pitch well under 1 mm) covering roughly the top ~15 mm of the front face. When installed this patch overlaps the rail's ribbed face and visually blends the hook into the rail. The ridges face the room, so they are cosmetic, not a grip surface.
  ZH: **正面锯齿区** —— 细密水平棱纹（间距远小于 1 mm），覆盖正面顶部约 15 mm。安装后这块区域正好叠在挂条的棱纹面上，使挂钩在视觉上融入挂条。棱纹朝向房间一侧，是装饰性的，不是摩擦面。
- EN: **Mounting claw (back side)** — below the plate top, a bridge arm projects rearward (toward the wall) from the plate, then turns down into a rear finger, forming a downward-opening claw. The gap between finger and plate is the slot that swallows the rail's top lip. Front-to-back order when installed: plate (room side) → slot (rail lip sits here) → finger (wall side). Outer corners are slightly rounded; the finger tip appears lightly chamfered/rounded.
  ZH: **安装爪（背面）** —— 在主板顶部下方，一条 bridge arm（顶桥）从主板向后（朝墙）伸出，再向下折成后爪指，构成开口朝下的爪。爪指与主板之间的缝隙就是吞住挂条上唇的槽。安装后从前到后的顺序：主板（房间侧）→ 槽（挂条上唇在此）→ 爪指（墙侧）。外侧转角略有圆角；爪指末端似有轻微倒角/圆角。
- EN: **Back wall pad** — below the rail zone the back face bulges 0.8 mm TOWARD the wall (total 3.0 mm thick, D13) and runs down into the J. It braces against the wall panel and carries the horizontal reaction H — the original's built-in answer to the tipping moment. Together with the cap it implies the panel surface sits ≈0.8 mm behind the rail face.
  ZH: **背面抵墙凸台** —— 挂条区以下，背面向墙面方向凸出 0.8 mm（总厚 3.0 mm，D13），向下延伸并入 J 钩。它抵住墙板、承担水平反力 H —— 是原件对倾覆力矩的自带解决方案。结合小帽可推出：墙板面比挂条正面靠后约 0.8 mm。
- EN: **J-hook** — the shank sweeps into a J curve opening up-and-away from the wall; the section appears to thicken gradually toward the tip end of the curve. A small V-notch is visible on the inner face right at the tip in the bare-hook photo (possibly a retention nib for loops/strings, possibly a cutting artifact — unconfirmed).
  ZH: **J 钩** —— 杆身向下弯成 J 形曲线，开口朝上并远离墙面；截面似乎向钩尖一端逐渐加厚。裸钩照片中钩尖内侧面可见一个小 V 形缺口（可能是防止挂绳滑落的小卡口，也可能是切割痕迹 —— 未确认）。

## Mounting Interaction（安装配合）

EN: To install, the claw drops over the rail's top edge from the front: the rail's top lip enters the 2.75 mm slot, the 2.4 mm finger hides behind the lip inside the rail cavity, and the plate's smooth back face rests on the lip's front face. Load on the J-hook torques the hook so the lower shank presses toward the wall — self-locking, no fasteners. The serrated patch overlaps the rail's ribbed face for visual continuity. See [mounting-rail](mounting-rail.md).
ZH: 安装时，安装爪从前方扣到挂条顶缘上：挂条上唇进入 2.75 mm 的槽，2.4 mm 的爪指藏进上唇后方的挂条腔体，主板光滑的背面贴在上唇正面。J 钩受载产生的力矩使下段杆身压向墙面 —— 自锁，无需紧固件。锯齿区叠在挂条棱纹面上，保持视觉连续。见 [mounting-rail](mounting-rail.md)。
